About Us

At Rapids Rafting Company, we are passionate about adventure and the great outdoors. For over 100 years, we have been dedicated to providing unforgettable rafting experiences for adventurers of all skill levels. We believe that every trip is an opportunity to connect with nature, challenge yourself, and create lasting memories with friends and family. Our commitment to safety and environmental sustainability ensures that you can enjoy the thrill of the rapids while preserving the beauty of the rivers we love. Join us for an exhilarating journey down some of the most stunning waterways, where laughter, camaraderie, and the spirit of adventure await!

History

Rapids Rafting Company was founded by Walter Rapids in 1865 when he fell into a rushing river in a logging accident. As he was pulled down the river, he climbed on top of a log and actually had a good time. So he decided to monetize it.
